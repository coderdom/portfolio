Annual Reports

I love annual reports! From flushing out the design of the cover, MD& and Notes to Financials to building out the financial tables themselves. Over the years, I've learned to ensure consistency from page-to-page and column to column. Utilizing style sheets, I am able to perform the task quickly and with spot on consistency. Those same style sheets allow me to make global changes to a design within minutes.

Many of the annual reports displayed in this portfolio have won awards.
